Play Frogger

Instructions

Your goal is to move the forg accross the screen. reaching the end of the screen moves you to the next level where the cars move faster, and grants you Current Level * 10 points. If you hit a car or fall in water you lose a life and if you lose 3 you get GAME OVER

Controls

w, up arrow = move up

s, down arrow = move down

d, right arrow = move right

p = pause
